Redfly provides collaborative people tracking for teams.

By connecting to your email it can find people that you communicate with and intelligently tag them.

Every few days it will send you an update containing people you've newly emailed or responded to so you can review the collected info and add your own notes.

You'll be able to view the curated contacts of other team members. No one can use your contacts without getting your permission first.
